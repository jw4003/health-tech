About HealthTech.co

HealthTech.co is a digital health management application designed to support individuals in monitoring, analysing, and improving their daily health and wellness. The system provides users with a centralized platform to record key health metrics, visualise trends through interactive dashboards, and gain insights into their physical wellbeing.

The application integrates modern mobile development technologies with a secure backend infrastructure to ensure efficient data processing and reliable storage of user information. Through features such as health metric tracking, device integration simulation, and data visualisation tools, HealthTech.co aims to promote proactive health management and informed decision-making.
